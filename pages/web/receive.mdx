# Receiving Funds

To receive funds, you simply need to provide your address to the sender. Typically, you will provide the default address for the account. Remember that in Penumbra, your wallet has multiple accounts, each with a separate balance.

## Get an address in the Frontend

To get an address in the frontend, you can use the **Receive** tab on the [Send](https://app.penumbra.zone/#/send) page:

![Receive](/images/receive.png)

## Get an address in Prax Wallet

Click the Prax Wallet extension icon in the Chrome toolbar and you will see your **Main Account** and its address:

![Prax Balances Default Account](/images/prax-balances.png)

Click the right arrow to see your other accounts and their default addresses.
